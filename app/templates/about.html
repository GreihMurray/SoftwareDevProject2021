Welcome to the CSCI 5030 - Software Development Spellchecker

This is a spellchecker that will allow you, the user, to input text and receive a quick, accurate spell check so you can be assured that all of your writing is free of errors!


How do I use it?

To use this tool, click your choice of language from the [dropdown/tab] menu, and then type or paste the text you want to check into the textbox below. When complete, press the spell check button to receive a list of words that may need corrections, or an output indicating your successful spellings.
